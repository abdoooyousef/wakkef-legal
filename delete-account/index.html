حذف حسابك

Two paths to remove your Wakkef! account and all associated data.

طريقتان لحذف حسابك في "وقف!" وكل البيانات المرتبطة بيه.

Option 1 · In-app deletion (recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is from inside the app — one tap, no waiting, no email back-and-forth.

  1. 1 Open Wakkef! on your device.
  2. 2 Tap the Settings icon (top-right of the home screen).
  3. 3 Scroll down to the Account section.
  4. 4 Tap Delete account.
  5. 5 Confirm by tapping Delete forever in the dialog.

What happens immediately:

Your profile, every custom level you have made, all your scores, in-game inventory, redeemed promo codes, and the Firebase Auth account itself are wiped within seconds.

Option 2 · Email request

If you cannot access the app (e.g. uninstalled it, or signed out and forgot your credentials), email us and we will delete your account manually.

Send an email to: ayn.developer@gmail.com

Subject line: Delete my Wakkef account

Please include in the email body:

  • The email address linked to your Google sign-in (so we can find your account)
  • Approximate date you started playing (helps locate guest accounts)
  • Any custom level titles you remember (optional, helps verify identity)

We will confirm deletion by email within 7 business days. The actual data wipe happens within 30 days from the request, per Google Play and GDPR requirements.

What is NOT deleted

The following remain after account deletion because they do not contain personal identifiers tied to you:

  • Anonymous gameplay analytics — aggregate session counts and level completion rates with no link back to your identity.
  • Anonymized moderation history — if any of your custom levels were ever reviewed for inappropriate content, the moderation record stays (anonymized) for our records.
