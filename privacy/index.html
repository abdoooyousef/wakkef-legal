1. Who we are

Wakkef! is a free mobile game developed and published by AYN Developer ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what information the app collects, how we use it, and your choices.

By installing or using Wakkef! you accept the terms of this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the app.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Account information (optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name from Google Sign-In. You can play Wakkef! fully anonymously without signing in — anonymous users are identified only by a random device-scoped ID that contains no personal information.

2.2 User-generated content

When you create custom levels inside "My Studio", the photos you upload and the AI-extracted cutouts are stored in our cloud (Firebase Storage) so you can play them on any device signed in to your account. Only you and AYN Developer (for moderation purposes) can access this content.

2.3 Gameplay data

Scores, levels completed, in-game inventory, achievements, and redeemed promo codes are saved to your account so you can resume on another device.

2.4 Diagnostics & crash reports

When the app crashes we collect anonymous crash logs and basic device information (Android version, device model, app version) via Firebase Crashlytics so we can fix bugs. No personal info is attached.

2.5 Advertising identifiers

The free version of Wakkef! shows ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your device settings. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads at any time from your device's privacy settings.

3. How we use the information

  • To run the game and sync progress across your devices.
  • To moderate inappropriate user-generated content.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve stability.
  • To display ads in the free version (via Google AdMob).
  • To respond to support requests you send us.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-party services

Wakkef! relies on the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to read:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage, Crashlytics.
  • Google AdMob — Advertising network for the free version.
  • Google Sign-In — Optional account login.

5. Data retention & deletion

You can delete your account at any time from inside the app: Settings → Account → Delete account. This removes your profile, custom levels, scores, and inventory within 30 days.

To request data deletion outside the app, email us at ayn.developer@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my Wakkef account".

6. Children

Wakkef! is rated for ages 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures (HTTPS for all transport, Firebase security rules, encryption at rest provided by Google Cloud) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
